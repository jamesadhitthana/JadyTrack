  • Does the app drain my battery?

    JadyTrack has the lowest battery drain of all locator apps on the market. We have an advanced algorithm that helps us decide when to wake up your phone and update your location—so we never just leave your GPS on. But, every app that uses location will use some extra battery. Most users should expect about 10% shorter battery life in a 24-hour period when running JadyTrack. This amount can vary significantly depending on how often you have the app open, how often you manually refresh a Circle Member's location, or how often you drive.

    For example, if one member is always, or frequently, checking on another member on the map, this will cause the member being located to experience higher battery drain. It will also drain more battery on the member who is checking because the app is open in the foreground. When the device is in transit, JadyTrack turns on high precision GPS in order to show detailed history driving routes which can drain battery over long periods of time (longer drives).

    We are always working to improve battery usage, so make sure you have the newest version of the app on your device.

  • What is check-in button?

    Check In allows you to send your location instantly to every member of your Circle with just the touch of a button!

    To send a "Check In":
    1. Tap the Check In button on the top right of the screen
    2. Choose a nearby place or current location that is listed
    3. If the recommended location is not exact, enter info in the box "Name Your Current Location"
    4. When you check in, the members in the Circle will receive a message of your location

  • How do i stop sharing my location?

    Turning off your location in one Circle will not affect location sharing in your other Circles. This lets you choose which Circles you want to share location in. NOTE: Pressing the Check In button will update your location in every Circle even if location sharing is turned off. If you press the SOS button, this will turn location sharing back on for that Circle.

    To stop sharing your location:
    1. Open the Menu
    2. In the app, tap on the Circle you want to edit
    3. Tap on "Location Sharing"
    4. Switch your Location Sharing to OFF
    5. On the map, your location will show the message "Location Paused"
