What is JADY?

JADY means James, Andre, Deananda, Yefta.

Four people full of passionate to solve problems that come in our surroundings through technology invention. It was our project when we were in college and we commit to focus in JadyTrack to become our lifetime project. Four of us focus to help people solve their issues by using applications that helps them.


JadyTrack understands that the core need of every family is the daily assurance of being safe and connected. That's why over 10 million families trust JadyTrack to provide them with everyday smart location sharing and protection during those unpredictable moments. From real time location notifications to roadside assistance to emergency detection and response, JadyTrack is creating the world's leading membership service.
